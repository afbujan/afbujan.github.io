Alejandro F. Bujan


Computational Neuroscientist

I am interested in understanding how the mechanisms that we use to process information from our environment and produce complex behavior are implemented in networks of highly specialized cells.


I am currently finishing my Ph.D. at the Bernstein Center for Computational Neuroscience Freiburg (BCF) under the supervision of Ad Aertsen and Arvind Kumar (check out my neurotree!).
